Constantine is roman style typeface inspired by emperor Constantine the Great.
He was born in nowadays Serbia, in city Naissus (Nish) and ruled from 306 to 337 AD.
Font is based on Cinzel with full serbian and cyrillic support, including the Chi Rho symbol.
